Agent capabilities

Two agents.
One thinks. One acts.

Mainthread runs two purpose-built agents. The Concierge manages context, communication, and decisions. The Task Executor handles real-world work on your behalf.

Concierge Agent
Your context layer.
  • Converts emails, messages, and voice notes into structured tasks
  • Sends you a daily briefing across all life domains
  • Monitors deadlines and flags what needs attention
  • Suggests what to delegate before you think to ask
  • Communicates via WhatsApp, email, or in-app chat
  • Learns your preferences and adapts over time
  • Proactively follows up with people you've delegated tasks to, and flags delays before they become problems
  • Hands tasks off to the executor when action is needed
Task Executor Agent
Your action layer.
  • Browses real websites and navigates complex web workflows
  • Fills and submits forms on your behalf
  • Books appointments, reservations, and services
  • Researches vendors, options, and pricing
  • Makes payments via prepaid virtual card — you set the budget v1
  • Pauses and requests your approval before sensitive actions
  • Returns a structured outcome report for every task
Different security principals

No access grants.
No integrations.
No exposure.

Concierge Agent

Has its own dedicated email address and WhatsApp number. You forward what it needs — it never touches your inbox, calendar, or accounts.

Task Executor Agent

Runs in a fully isolated browser session. For payments, it uses a prepaid virtual card with a budget cap you define — no connection to your financial accounts.

FAQ

Questions we get asked.

What counts as an agent task execution?

Each time an agent completes or attempts a delegated task on your behalf. Viewing, approving, or replying doesn't count toward your limit.

Can I change plans?

Yes, any time. Upgrades are immediate; downgrades apply at the next billing cycle. No penalties, no friction.

What happens if I hit the 50-execution limit on Delegate?

You'll be notified and can either upgrade to Autopilot or wait for your next monthly reset. Agents won't act without your knowledge.

Is my data private?

Yes. Each account is fully isolated. Agents operate on your behalf only, with your explicit approval for every sensitive action.

Do you offer refunds?

If you're not satisfied within your first 14 days, we'll refund you — no questions asked.

Does Mainthread work for non-technical people?

Absolutely. Mainthread is built for anyone juggling multiple life commitments — lawyers, parents, managers, freelancers. No technical background required.
